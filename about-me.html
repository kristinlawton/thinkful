Introduction

I'm a principal information architect with 18 years of experience helping non-profits succeed online. I've led large website redesigns for many of the largest non-profit organizations in the US and Canada.

My goal is to create online experiences that inspire and delight users and tell the story of our clients' work with beautiful visuals. We've won 2 Interactive Media Awards ("Best in Class") for non-profit website design in 2014 for www.feedingamerica.org and www.commoncause.org.

Other recent designs include:

  • Children's Hospital Colorado Foundation
  • Courage Classic
  • Courage Is

Our user experience-focused design methodology includes strategy and user research, stakeholder interviews, information architecture, wireframes, content strategy, CMS content modeling, technical specs, design, usability testing, development, QA, launch, training delivery and post-launch analysis.

I started my career with the non-profit Environmental Defense Fund in 1996 as a developer of the Webby award-winning environmental information service scorecard.org.

I was a founding member of GetActive Software in 2000, which grew from a start-up of 10 people to the 2nd largest eCRM provider to non-profit organizations in the US.
